# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

- Recruiters and hiring managers evaluating technical talent
- Fellow engineers exploring projects and aesthetic approaches  
- Design-conscious collaborators and project enthusiasts
- Future visitors discovering additional projects as portfolio grows

All visitors are in an **exploring** state of mind—browsing with curiosity, wanting to understand who Deanna is technically and aesthetically.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio that serves as both credential and creative statement. The design itself demonstrates technical capability and aesthetic sensibility. Success means visitors leave with a clear sense of Deanna's engineering excellence, unique visual identity, and desire to explore her work further or collaborate.

## Brand Personality

**Textured Boldness. Warm Precision. Cyber-Feminine.**

The interface should feel like stepping into a carefully curated Brooklyn studio—confident, warm, technically formidable, unapologetically expressive. Not cold corporate. Not generic SaaS. Not overly cutesy. Distinctly feminine in a powerful, tech-forward way.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing page templates (icon + 3 features grids)
- Cold, corporate aesthetics (sterile whites, harsh blues)
- Dark mode tech-bro vibes (neon on black, aggressive gradients)
- Overly cutesy/pastel aesthetics
- Bootstrap-style sectioned layouts with clear breaks

## Design Principles

1. **Tactile over glass** — Textured surfaces with depth, not generic glassmorphism
2. **Bold over safe** — Saturated colors, dramatic typography, confident choices
3. **Flow over sections** — One continuous editorial experience, not segmented pages
4. **Material over flat** — Warm shadows, fabric textures, physical presence
5. **Iridescent as identity** — Your gradient colors are the hero, not accents

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion support for vestibular disorders. Keyboard navigation. Screen reader friendly structure despite bold visuals.
